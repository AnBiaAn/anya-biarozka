Anya Biarozka is a Berlin-based Senior Product Designer, Creative Director, and systems thinker with 10+ years of experience across automotive, energy, finance, healthcare, and emerging technologies.

She works at the intersection of product strategy, user experience, brand systems, and AI — combining systems thinking with hands-on design leadership to help founders and established organizations navigate complexity, uncover opportunities, and build experiences that create value for both users and businesses.

From wearables and AI-powered products to fintech, healthcare, and context-driven systems, Anya helps startups bring ambitious ideas to life and supports established organizations through transformation and innovation. By connecting user needs, business strategy, and emerging technologies, she designs scalable products, services, and ecosystems that drive adoption, growth, and lasting impact.
